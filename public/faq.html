FAQs

Are your teas organic?

Yes, we offer a selection of organic teas. Look for the "Organic" label on the product description to identify our organic blends.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Please note that shipping options and delivery times may vary depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you encounter any issues or have concerns, please reach out to our customer support team, and we will gladly assist you.

Are your teas suitable for people with dietary restrictions?

We provide detailed ingredient lists and allergen information for each tea blend. However, if you have specific dietary concerns, we recommend consulting the ingredient list or contacting our customer support team for more information.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk ordering?

Yes, we have wholesale and bulk ordering options available for businesses and retailers. Please contact our wholesale team for more information and to discuss your specific requirements.

Are your tea blends suitable for children?

Our tea blends are generally intended for adult consumption. If you have specific concerns or questions regarding the suitability of our teas for children, we recommend consulting a healthcare professional.
